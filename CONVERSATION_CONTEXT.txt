## DESIGN DECISIONS
- Theme DNA system: each theme has borderRadius, borderStyle, cardStyle, shadowStyle, fontHeading/Body/Data, animSpeed/Style, texture
- Style resolver (utils/styles.js): components call cardStyle(theme), headingStyle(theme) etc. instead of hardcoding glass/blur patterns
- FlagIcon component: maps team names to ISO codes, renders SVGs from flagcdn.com
- Learning mode: independent toggle (not tied to Lite/Pro), adds inline educational badges
- Alerts: live alerts shown first, demo alerts fill remaining spots (always 5+ visible)
- Polling: 15s interval, 0.05% threshold for alert generation, 0.3% for confetti
